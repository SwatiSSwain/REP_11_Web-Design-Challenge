Max Temperature

As seen in the above scatter plot, temperature appears to have a strong correlation in the Northern Hemisphere (Lat >= 0) and since a good-fit line drawn amongst these points would have a negative slope, that correlation is negative. So, in Northern hemisphere the temperature decreases with increase in latitude. In the Southern hemisphere, the slope is not as steep. The temperature variance is between 60 to 100 F for latitudes ranges -60 to 0. The further we travel in the Southern hemisphere, the tempature will decrease a little bit.
